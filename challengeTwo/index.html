A Variation of the Game "21"

Rules:

  • Get the closest to 21 without going over.
  • The computer will draw again if their total is 11 or less.
  • Choose to draw another card, or pass.
  • Game ends when you pass or your total is over 21.
  • The winner is the one whose sum of cards is the closest to 21 without going over.

Card Values:

  • Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever helps the most)
  • King, Queen, Jack = 0
  • 2-10 = face value
